BHARATHANATYAM

Rangabharana is conducting Dance, Music, Classes, seasoned teachers who have mastery over Bharathanatyam, Vocal and instrumental music impart training to the promising child prodigies. Students of Rangabharana take part in the annual Music, Dance, Drama Examinations conducted by the kamataka govt.. Many children cameout with flying colours in these examinations and brought reputation, name and fame to the institution.

WESTERN / FILMY DANCE

Apart from Classical Indian art forms, Training in Western Dances, Free Style. Filmy and contemporary dance forms too are imparted here at Rangabharana. Children who have undergone training here are blossomming as beautiful flowers and Rangabharana feels proud about them. The up coming artistes trained here have already shown their talent by performing in TV serials and films and thus brought name to this art centre. Another fascinating feature of the center is that those children who were trained here have already made a mark in the field and successfully running once own art schools. Thus the fragrance of Rangabharana is spreading to nook and corner. In order to exhibit the talent of the budding artists, the art school organises 'Talent Show' twice in a Year and this has drawn the attention of the art lovers.

Chandrapriya Auditorium

Olong left need of the residents of the northern part of Bangalore to have Art Theatre has at last been fulfilled. The Lakshman couple of the 'Bhavani Trust' came forward to give financial aid to Rangabharana to build an art theatre at Sanjaynagar. The Theatre has since been built and it is with High technology well equipped with light and sound systems. The two hundred seating capacity of Chandrapriya Rangamandira is located at 7"main, 3"Cross, K.E.B. Layout, Sanjaynagar, Bangalore. This beautiful Art Theatre is bound to cater to the residents' cultural activities. The budding artists of Rangabharana will stage at least two programmes in a month at the Kalamandira. The Rangabharana intends to invite reputed artists to perform on this modern stage. The theatre is an apt location to organise seminars. workshops and other literary activities. The artistes of Rangabharana will perform "Ranga Sanje", an evening of art performance atleast twice a month. This beautiful locale is indeed value addition to the cultural environ of Sanjaynagar.
